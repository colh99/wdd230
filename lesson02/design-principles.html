PARC: Contrast

Hello Games

nomanssky.com

The website for the video game No Man's Sky exemplifies how to use contrast to draw the user's eyes. The dark background makes the game's artwork and screenshots pop out. It also allows for strong readability. Additionally, despite their small size, you will almost immediately notice the bright orange colored buttons that link you to places to purchase the game.

White Space, Clean Design

Tesla

tesla.com

Tesla's website is a great example of White Space and Clean Design. In this case, the photos used are very important: they are simple and clean, which helps round out the whole design. In turn, the design leaves an impression that the product is also clean and stylish. The user is given straightforward options, and the product is the primary focus.

Visual Hierarchy

Spotify

spotify.com

If you want your customers to take the path you want, you need to make the path obvious. Spotify does this by putting a single button at the front and center of their home page, with an attractive offer. They understand that they have extensive brand recognition, so they don't waste time explaining who they are. Instead, they jump straight to business.
