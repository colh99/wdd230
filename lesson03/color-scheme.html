Color Scheme

These are the colors that will be used for the site.

#ff8c00 The primary color. Chosen to match the clouds at sunset, and captures Cloud City's unique identity.
#4d4d4d The secondary color. Chosen to contrast the primary orange, while being symbolic of Cloud City's industry.
#FFD199 A muted version of the primary color. Used to compliment the primary without being too flashy.
#000000 Black. This will be used for most of the body text, and will provide some much-needed simplicity to round out the overall design.
