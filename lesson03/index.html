Cloud City Commerce

cloudcitycommerce.com

Site Purpose

The Cloud City Chamber of Commerce would like to promote the city's two main industries: Tibanna gas mining, and tourism. The site will need to attract people from across the galaxy who are looking for new opportunites, or a blissful getaway. Such individuals should be able to find information relevant to these purposes, and be able to contact the Chamber from the site. The end goal is that such individuals will be able to benefit from visiting or relocating to Cloud City, which will help advance our economy and thus promote the prosperity of the city as a whole.
