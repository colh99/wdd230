Cloud City Commerce

cloudcitycommerce.com

Site Purpose

The Cloud City Chamber of Commerce would like to promote the city's two main industries: Tibanna gas mining, and tourism. The site will need to attract people from across the galaxy who are looking for new opportunites, or a blissful getaway. Such individuals should be able to find information relevant to these purposes, and be able to contact the Chamber from the site.
