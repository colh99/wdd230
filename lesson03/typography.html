Typography

The font of choice for the body text is called Work Sans, as it communicates a sense of prosperity, thus enhancing the theme of industrial success while maintaing a luxurious feel. Line height will be 1.5em to improve readablity and cleanliness of the design. Additionally, most body text will be in black.

The header and footers, as well as the h2 headings, will be in the Orbitron font, which is a familiar style to many people across the galaxy. The text at the header and footer will be white against their orange backgrounds, but the h2 headings will remain black with the rest of the body text. The h2 headings will also recieve 1px additional letter spacing, while the h1 will recieve 2px letter spacing, to improve readablity of the font.
