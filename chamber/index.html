News Release

Tibanna gas production and exports have increased by a steady 7% this quarter, in line with the goals of the Cloud City Miner's Guild. This trend is expected to continue for the next 2-3 years, coinciding with the construction of new refinery platforms. It was previously announed that 5 new platforms would be constructed by the end of the year, with an additional 8 to begin construction next year.

A portion of profits gained from these exports will be reinvested into the city, in accordance with the deal brokered between the Cloud City Miner's Guild and the Chamber of Commerce.
